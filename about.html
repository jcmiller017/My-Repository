ABOUT ME
Hi everyone! For those who don't know, I'm JoJo from Chatham, NJ, and I'm a senior Marketing and Supply Chain Management major at the University of Maryland. At school, I am very involved with my sorority, Alpha Xi Delta, and have held the positions of Philanthropy Chair and VP of Programs over the past two years. This foundation helped me develop my career aspirations, as I aspire to work in event planning in either the entertainment or retail industry, utilizing my organization skills, my logistics knowledge, and outgoing personality.
